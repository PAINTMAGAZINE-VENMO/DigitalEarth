The ConStitution of the WikiBased digital republic of NooPolis:
(For Questions: NooPolisFaqEn)

!Principle
* All CitiZens have FreeDom.

!Citizenship
* All CitiZens have exactly one MindId and therewith a login to the MindWiki.
* With receiving a MindId you become a Citizen and you accept the ConStitution.
* CitiZens are on a first-name basis.
* CitiZens are intertwingled into WebOfTrust.
* Existing CitiZens can invite new CitiZens.
* For registration, the inviter has to choose the first name, surname and the gender of the invited.
* Bofore inviting, the inviter has to create a profile page in the MindWiki.
* The inviter has to choose the correct first name and surname of the invited.
* The profile page should have the correct CamelCase spelling of the  first name and surname. Valid exceptions are:
** short versions of the first name (eG Tina instead of Martina)
** registered artist names (eG AlbanNikolaiHerbst)
* A subsequent name change is subject to verification by at least two Trustees.
* For the case of EndOfLife of CitiZens, they can declare their LastWill. Here they can inherit their virtual assets.
* If no LastWill was declared, the virtual fortune will go to the BankAccount of the PieschenBank. 

!ConStitution and Legislation
* All rules of the ConStitution and other pages of the MindWiki may be changed.
* No rule change applies retroactively.
* There is the AllEyes principle.
* Changes of the ConStitution are considered to be adopted by all citizens, if no citizen objects to a change within a veto respite by editing the wiki contrary.
* The veto respite is 3 days. 
* If CitiZens are longer absent, they can extend the period of absence to 14 days.
* In the case of a disagreements the KabiNett can issue a MindVote among all citizens.
* At the MindVote all citizens decide, whose BankAccount is at least 1000 KayGroschen. The simple majority decides.
* Every citizen shall once a month participate in the UrWahl.
* In case of a violation of the ConStitution, imposition of penalties or order restricting the FreeDom of citizens have sought.
* The MindCourt meets a judgment by MindVote of all CitiZens.
* The FreeDom of CitiZens can only be restricted by a ruling of the MindCourt.
* All of our texts are subject to GnuFdl. 

!Economy
* NooPolis is a VirtuelleOekonomie. 
* The currency of NooPolis are KayGroschen. 
* The PieschenBank takes care of them.
* The money supply is 10.000.000 KayGroschen.
* CitiZens have a BankAccount.
* All transactions of the BankAccount are transparent to all CitiZens.
* The savings and spendings of NooPolis are managed in the BudGet. 
* Benefits are paid in KayGroschen .
* In the Territory, active companies are listed in the TradeRegister.
* The Shares of each company can be traded on the MindFloor.
* MindBroker is a people's owned operation on the sovereign territory of NooPolis.
* Mind Broker is the e-government service provider of NooPolis and guardian of MindId's.
* NooPolis promotes regional economic systems.
* For supra regional cash flows, a TobinTax is charged.
* CitiZens can declare a TobinTaxVote on their profile page.
* If CitiZens have not declared TobinTaxVote, their TobinTaxVote counts as "0%".
* The amount of current TobinTax is calculated as the arithmetic mean of all TobinTaxVote's.
* Transactions on the BankAccount, the MindFloor, the IpoKurs and the BudGet will be stored up to the end of life of NooPolis.
* The EndOfLife of NooPolis is 2008-12-31. 

!Government
* NooPolis is a republic with the LeaderShip model of PrimusInterPares.
* CitiZens once per year per KabiNettWahl elect the KabiNett of NooPolis.
* The KabiNett leads the ongoing operations of NooPolis.
* All members of the KabiNett's document their future actions (DoIt)
* All members of the KabiNett's lead a list of tasks, which they always return (ImmerWieder).
* All members of the KabiNett always read the KabiNettChat at least every 3 days.
* At least once a week invites a member of the Cabinet to SprechStunde.
* All members of the KabiNett receive a monthly payment of 10,000 KayGroschen of PieschenBank.
* The KabiNett sent once a month the newsletter.
* CitiZens need to receive the newsletter via EinSchreiben.
* If CitiZens inside three times in a row to receive the newsletter not confirm, their citizenship status expires and the VerMoegen on their BankAccount goes to the PieschenBank.
