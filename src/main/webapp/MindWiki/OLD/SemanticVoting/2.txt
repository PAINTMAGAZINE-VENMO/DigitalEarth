* HerKunft: "Voting is a method for a group such as a meeting or an electorate to make a decision or express an opinion" - [WikiPedia:Voting]

A SemanticWeb vocabulary for votings within the SocialGraph:
* Each voting has a unique URL
* eligible to vote are CitiZens of the UnitedSemanticNations who are reachable inside the SocialGraph via "foaf:knows" from a voting committee seed list.
* the CitiZens vote by publishing an RDF resource which expresses their voting as an RDF statement.
