LouisenStrasse LockSchuppen CoWorking

LockOffice is the starting point of a creative CoWorking Space in StadtDresden, where entrepreneurs, designers, programmers and other people interested in collaborative work ways will meet.

Office space for daily and monthly work will be provided. Facilities for workshops (web and IT tools making collaborative work more effective and relaxing).

Check out our [Tweets|http://twitter.com/LockSchuppen] whether we are in and drop in.
