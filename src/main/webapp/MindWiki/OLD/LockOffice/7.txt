LouisenStrasse LockSchuppen CoWorking

LockOffice is the starting point of a creative CoWorkingSpace in StadtDresden, where EntrePreneur's, designers, ProGram'mers and other people interested in collaborative work ways can meet.

Office and community space for Entrepreneur's on hourly, daily and monthly basis are provided. Facilities for WorkShop's and CoLearning (web and IT tools making collaborative work more effective and relaxing).

Check out current status of office attendance via [HallenProjekt|http://hallenprojekt.de]
