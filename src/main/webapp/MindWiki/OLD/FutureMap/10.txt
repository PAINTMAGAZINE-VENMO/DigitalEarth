* MindTags: MitMachSache

Die Grundidee der FutureMap: ZuKunft greifbarer machen.
Die Folgen des AcCelerate'ing Change für das eigene Leben vor Augen führen.

We want to dream, InVent and PreDict the future of NooPolis, PlanetEarth and the entire NooSphere.

Therefore we use FutureCard's, which are arranged on a TimeLine by our CitiZens.
Each FutureCard has:
* a page in the MindWiki (with a unique PageName)
* a clear and simple one line PreDict'ion.
* a CardOwner who is the "human voice and face" of this card.
* a CardHolder who is currently holding the physical card. If the CardOwner is not the CardHolder, the FutureCard has to be sent to the CardOwner __AsAp__ via MindPost. 
* an "UnTil"-value which has to be defined by the CardOwner

Soon we will start to discuss causal dependencies between different FutureCard's.
Soon, CitiZens will be able to express their ErWartung about the "UnTil"-Date of a FutureCard.
Soon, the position of a FutureCard must be consistent with the average PreDict'ions
of CitiZens.
