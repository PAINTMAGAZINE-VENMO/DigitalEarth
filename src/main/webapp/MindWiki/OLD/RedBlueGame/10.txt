The RedBlueGame works as follows:

We
# take a list of interesting topics and/or MindPeople from a nearby wiki or website,
# TransForm the topics the into CamelCase
# compare which of topics are already in the PageIndex (=blue)

Examples:
* AcceleratingFuturePeopleDatabase
* AgiPioneer
* ElektrischerReporter
* ForbesList
* FrankfurterTreppe
* GermanysBestWebFounder
* OvercomingBias
* PayPalMafia
* PenguiCon
* SiggiBecker
* SingularitySummit
* TrilateralCommission
* VanityFair
* WorkAtGoogle
* WorldGeniuses
