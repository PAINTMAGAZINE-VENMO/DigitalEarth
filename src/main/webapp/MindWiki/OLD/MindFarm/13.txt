The MindFarm is a small WikiBased BrowserGame for the VirtualEconomy of NooPolis.

Here is how it works:
* All CitiZens of NooPolis can play MindFarm.
* Our board is located around the LockSchuppen as you can see [here|http://maps.google.com/maps?q=http://noopolis.org/kml]
* you can buy land in the MindShop.

We have a small virtual field: PlanetErde

Layers:
* L0: 10 Degrees (18x36)
* L1: 1 Degree (10x10)
* L2: 0.1 Degrees
* L3: 0.01 Degrees
* L4: 0.001 Degrees
* L5: 0.0001 Degrees

The goal of the MindFarm-BrowserGame is to create a realistic digital model of PlanetErde.
