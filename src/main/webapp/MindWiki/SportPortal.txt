* IsA: ProJect

Idea of DanielPoodratchi and RainerWasserfuhr from 2006 to start a SocialNetwork similar to XingLe, but customized for active SportsPerson's, additionally supporting Clubs, Events likes Trainings and Matches or LeagueTable's.

ProJect abandonned in 2007 due to ComPetition.

Während andere noch an teure, starre Portale glauben, wäre mal ein semantisches SportWiki cool.
